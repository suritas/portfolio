Hi, thanks for visiting my portfolio!

A little about me: I recently received my Masters of Science in Information from the University of Michigan, in the Human Computer Interaction & User Experience Program.
My passion is research, which I have been doing for over 5 years, most recently through UX Consulting. I also have a passion for technical and educational writing, and I am curious how language can be combined with UX methodology to improve discoverability, particularly for mobile products.
I currently live in Seattle, Washington where I was born and raised. When I am not working, you can find me birdwatching, at Trader Joe's, or enjoying the Pacific Northwest's sights.  


My approach

My background in scientific research and technical education taught me to communicate complex processes with precision and care. With UI, I combine affordances and signifiers with concise language to guide the user. In practice, I frequently use information architecture, survey, qualitative & quantitative analysis, task observation, and preference testing to inform my design solutions.
