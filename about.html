Hi, there!

I’m a UX Research, UX Design, and Project Management professional with 8+ years total experience.
I manage enterprise technical & non-technical projects and conduct end-to-end UX Research & UX Design for Fortune 500s and start-ups like Floyd Home, Evva Health, NBC-Universal, CNBC. I’ve worked on platforms including mobile (iOS & Android), smart-devices, voice, and cross-platform and AI-driven integrations.
I hold a Masters of Science in Information: User Experience & Human Computer Interaction from the University of Michigan and a GLSS Lean Six Sigma Black Belt.
Find me “in the zone” developing research protocols, conducting analyses, and prototyping in Figma.
Outside work you can find me enjoying a good book, playing badminton, or enjoying the vistas and restaurants of the Pacific Northwest.   


My approach

My work is guided by User Experience principles, Design Thinking, and Agile/Lean methodologies.
With a half decade in Research, and a half decade in Project Management & Coordination I am able to efficiently turn research findings into prototyped features. My process utilizes...
(1) Scientific & Lean/Agile Principles to develop a cohesive research protocol
(2) Project Management Tools including Smartsheet, Microsoft Planner, Miro, and Sharepoint to track progress aligned to timelines
(3) Mixed-method (Qualitative, Quantitative, and Observational) Research Methods such as competitive/comparative analysis, affinity mapping, heuristic evaluation, contextual inquiry interviews, information architecture, process & pain point mapping, survey, task observation, preference testing, and A/B testing
(4) Analysis & Data Tools such as Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, Microsoft Excel, Nvivo, Visio, Lucidchart, and Miro to reveal actionable findings.
(5) Design & Prototyping Tools including Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop for creative data-driven solutions.
