Hi, there!

I am a Qualitative Research, Prototyping, and Project Management professional with 8+ years managing technical & non-technical projects, consulting experience in UX Research & Design, and a Masters in User Experience & Human Computer Interaction. My passion is bridging the gap between research and design through cutting-edge UX Methods and Lean Project Management. I enjoy working with day-to-day and emerging platforms including mobile, smart (voice), and multi-device integration products. Find me "in the zone" whenever I am putting together a research protocol or conducting qualitative research/analysis, implementing for process/product improvements or designing and presenting interactive prototypes.
When I am not working, you can find me birdwatching, playing badminton, or simply enjoying the Pacific Northwest's natural vistas.   


My approach

My work is guided by UX-R (User Experience Research), Design Thinking, and Process Improvement principles from the University of Michigan's prestigious Masters of Science in Information and the Lean Six Sigma Project Management certification.
My background in research, technical project management, and database training facilitation allows me to communicate complex processes with precision and care. Both for users and for stakeholders and clients.
In practice, I frequently use scientific and lean methods to develop a research protocol, and conduct tailored qualitative & quantitative analyses such as competitive/comparative analysis, affinity mapping, heuristic evaluation, contextual inquiry interviews, information architecture, process & pain point mapping, survey, task observation, and preference & A/B testing to inform my design solutions. I typically prototype using Figma and Adobe XD, creating elements with Adobe Illustrator.
